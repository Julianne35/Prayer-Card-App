Luke 17:6 NIV , Pray the Scripture Father God, I thank You Lord, as it is written in Your Word, I am given a measure of faith to exercise. Remind me today, by the authority of Your Word, by the blood of Jesus, I can take my gifted faith and command the mountains of worry, stress, fear, lack, poverty, over whelming emotions, & anything else that is not of Your nature (love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, meekness and self control) to be removed. Remind me who I am in You. You tell me in Your Word, fear not for I have overcome the world. Help me to stand on the Rock of Jesus, and to not be moved, to trust in You and You alone.
